7.

Sustainable value chain

Our value chain relations include factors that go beyond financial gains. Given our position in the sector, we believe that Movida is responsible for promoting good practices and dialog aligned around sustainability trends. In light of this, our Sustainability Policy sets out sustainable purchasing criteria for the processes of selecting and procuring suppliers.

With the car manufacturers in our supply chain representing the lion's share of supplier expenditure, we have partnered with the Carbon Disclosure Project (CDP) in order to provide online training, with the support of the CDP Supply Chain Risks and Climate Opportunities questionnaire.

9.

Climate change and environmental agenda

Withstanding climate change and comprehensively managing carbon are our focus

Spearheading this movement, we became a B Corporation – the second listed Brazilian company to grace this ecosystem. Also confirming our focus on good practices in this agenda, we issued sustainability-linked bonds, in what was an unprecedented move in the sector worldwide, committing to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 30% by 2030. In 2021, we also remained in the B3 Corporate Sustainability Index (ISE) for the third consecutive year, also joining the Efficient Carbon Index (B3 ICO2).
